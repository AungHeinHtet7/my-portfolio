Privacy Policy for NeoVerse Facebook App

This Privacy Policy describes how the NeoVerse Facebook App ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our App.

Data We Access

The NeoVerse Facebook App requires the following Facebook Page management permissions to function:

  • pages_show_list: To display a list of Facebook Pages that you manage.
  • pages_read_engagement: To read content and other engagement data for the Facebook Pages you manage.
  • pages_manage_posts: To create, edit, and delete posts on the Facebook Pages you manage.

How We Use Your Data

The App uses these permissions exclusively for the purpose of automated posting to the NeoVerse Facebook Page. This functionality allows for efficient management and scheduling of content on the designated Facebook Page.

Data Collection, Storage, and Sharing

We want to be clear: the NeoVerse Facebook App does not collect, store, or share any personal user data or any data obtained through the Facebook API with third parties. All operations are performed in real-time to facilitate automated posting, and no information is retained beyond the immediate execution of these tasks.
