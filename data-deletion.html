Data Deletion Instructions - NeoVerse

The NeoVerse Facebook App does not collect, store, or retain any personal user data. All operations are performed in real-time and no information is saved on our servers.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to request the deletion of any data associated with your use of the NeoVerse App, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to aungheinhtet.me@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your Facebook username or the email address associated with your Facebook account.
  3. We will process your request and confirm the deletion within 30 days.

Removing App Access from Your Facebook Account

You can also remove the NeoVerse App's access to your Facebook account at any time:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings.
  2. Navigate to "Security and Login" > "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "NeoVerse" and click "Remove".

This will revoke all permissions granted to the NeoVerse App.
